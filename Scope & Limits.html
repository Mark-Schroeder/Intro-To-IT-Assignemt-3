Scope & Limits

Kaleid AI made excellent strides with assignment 2 by building the skeleton prototype painting the design and the navigation of their application. This was a great foundation to lay a proof of concept reinforcing the progressing skills of the team. The skeleton prototype was designed on Figma, a web-based vector graphic editor that allows users to collaborate application design (Figma 2022). At the end of A2 the only showcasing feature was design and navigation aligning the values with the applications most impressive feature, visualisation. Although the next phase of the plan is to develop something simple the team will work effortlessly to incorporate as many requirements possible treating all future states as an assembly line.
